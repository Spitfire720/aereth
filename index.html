The Shattering of Aereth

Aereth was once whole — a living world bound by continuity and meaning. When the fracture occurred, reality did not collapse violently. Instead, it forgot itself.

Cities exist half-remembered. Roads lead to absence. The land persists through fragments of intent left behind by those who vanished.

The World of Aereth

From the Verdant Spiral of Elderwyth to the burning sanctums of Pyrexis, Aereth is divided by regions that survived the fracture in different states.

Between them lies the Fractured Expanse — a scar of broken continuity where meaning decays.
